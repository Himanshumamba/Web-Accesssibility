Your Trusted Partner in Personalized Group Travel

Real travel begins when the planning ends and you can finally just be there. At LABUSA Travel, we make that happen. We're a full-service travel management company handling flights, accommodations, tours, and every logistical detail from start to finish. Specialized in personalized international group tours across the Middle East and Africa, we create journeys that go beyond typical sightseeing.

Whether you're organizing faith-based travel, family group travel, or corporate group tours, you'll have time to connect deeply with local cultures, travel at your own rhythm, and return home with memories that stay close to your heart. No matter where your heart leads you next, we'll help you get there with expertise and thorough planning.

A Journey Through Ghana, Ethiopia, Uganda, Kenya, and Egypt.

Echoes of Africa Tour:
January 8-23, 2027

Discover the heart of Africa on a cultural journey through Ghana, Ethiopia, Uganda, Kenya, and Egypt. From vibrant cities and ancient landmarks to wildlife encounters and meaningful heritage experiences, this adventure connects you with the history, spirit, and beauty of five unforgettable destinations. 

Travel with expert local guides, enjoy comfortable accommodations, and explore each country through immersive activities that bring its culture to life. 

Reserve your spot at a discounted rate with a deposit, and pay the remaining balance through a flexible payment plan.

•    15-Day: Jan 8–23 — Ghana → Ethiopia → Uganda → Kenya → Egypt
•    10-Day Option A: Jan 8–18 — Ghana → Ethiopia → Uganda
•    10-Day Option B: Jan 14–23 — Uganda → Kenya → Egypt
•    Custom: TBD — Select dates and locations
