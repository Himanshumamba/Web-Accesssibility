About Himanshu

I am a web accessibility specialist focused on helping businesses improve accessibility for websites, applications, and PDFs. My work includes WCAG reviews, remediation guidance, and practical accessibility fixes.

I have completed 100+ projects and work across issues related to WCAG 2.2, ADA expectations, PDF accessibility, keyboard access, screen reader support, and accessible content structure.

I do not only report issues. I also help teams understand what is broken, why it matters to users, and what needs to change in code, design, or content.

If you serve users in the United States and need an accessibility expert who can work remotely, this site is built to make that easy.
