Website Accessibility Audit Service Explained

A website accessibility audit helps identify issues that prevent users with disabilities from accessing your website. These issues may include missing alt text, poor color contrast, keyboard navigation problems, and screen reader compatibility issues.

Fixing accessibility issues improves usability, helps meet WCAG guidelines, and reduces the risk of ADA compliance problems. Businesses that serve users in the United States often need accessibility audits to support legal compliance and improve user experience.

My accessibility audit service provides clear issue reports, practical fix recommendations, and optional remediation support to help you improve your website quickly.
